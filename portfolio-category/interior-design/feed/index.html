Art Direction - Design

Spring Blossom Cottage

Client

James Parker

Service

Full Interior Design

Date

May 2017

The Idea

Re-imagining the Future of Space

The concept for the Spring Blossom Cottage focuses on bringing the freshness and vibrancy of spring indoors. The goal is to create a cheerful, light-filled space that feels rejuvenating and full of life. The design will incorporate pastel colors, floral patterns, and natural materials to evoke the essence of springtime.

— We started with

Kitchen Design

The kitchen will be designed to be both functional and charming. It will include white shaker-style cabinetry, a butcher block countertop, and vintage-style appliances in pastel hues. An island with a marble top and bar stools will provide additional seating and workspace. Open shelving will display colorful dishware and decorative items, adding a personal touch. The color scheme will complement the living area, ensuring a cohesive look throughout the space.

— The Fundamentals

Bedroom Design

The bedroom will be a serene retreat, filled with the freshness of spring. A white wrought-iron bed with floral bedding will be the focal point, accompanied by nightstands with delicate lamps. Soft, luxurious bedding in pastel tones will ensure comfort. A large wardrobe with painted floral details will provide ample storage space. A cozy reading nook with a comfortable chair and a small bookshelf will offer a quiet space to relax.

— Product Design

Bathroom Design

The bathroom will combine elegance with a touch of whimsy. Key features will include a clawfoot bathtub, a vanity with a marble countertop, and a large mirror with a floral frame. The use of mosaic tiles in soft pastel colors will add a touch of springtime charm. The color palette will be light and fresh, with soft lighting to create a spa-like atmosphere. Fresh flowers and botanical prints will complete the look.

Client Feedback

Interioz transformed our cottage into a beautiful Spring Blossom haven. Their attention to detail and ability to capture the essence of spring was amazing. The final result is a bright, cheerful space that feels like a breath of fresh air. We are absolutely thrilled with our new home

James Parker

Coastal Chic Retreat

Client

Michael Smith

Service

Full Interior Design

Date

June 2007

The Idea

Re-imagining the Future of Space

The concept for the Coastal Chic Retreat focuses on bringing the serenity and charm of coastal living into an urban apartment. The goal is to create a relaxing, beach-inspired space that feels light, airy, and refreshing. The design will incorporate natural materials, a soothing color palette, and nautical elements to evoke the feeling of a seaside escape.

— We started with

Kitchen Design

The kitchen will be designed to be both practical and visually appealing. It will include white shaker-style cabinetry, a quartz countertop with subtle veining, and stainless steel appliances. A kitchen island with wicker bar stools will provide additional seating and a touch of coastal charm. Open shelving will display coastal dishware and glassware. The color scheme will mirror the living area, ensuring a seamless transition between spaces.

— The Fundamentals

Bedroom Design

The bedroom will be a tranquil retreat, reminiscent of a beachside cottage. A four-poster bed with a canopy of flowing white fabric will be the focal point, complemented by nightstands made from driftwood. Soft, airy bedding in shades of white and blue will ensure comfort. A vintage-style armoire will provide ample storage. A window seat with cushions will offer a cozy nook for reading or relaxing, enhancing the serene atmosphere.

— Product Design

Bathroom Design

The bathroom will combine coastal elegance with modern amenities. Key features will include a freestanding bathtub with a view, a vanity with a marble countertop, and a large mirror framed in driftwood. The use of mosaic tiles in shades of blue will add a touch of the ocean. The color palette will be light and airy, with soft lighting to create a calming, spa-like atmosphere.

Art Direction - Design

Serene Coastal Retreat

Concept design establishes a foundational framework that steers the development and realization of a project, ensuring a cohesive and well-guided evolution from vision to completion.

Client:

Michael and Laura Thompson

Date:

June 2009

Re-imagining the Future of Space

Idea

The Serene Coastal Retreat aims to capture the essence of a peaceful seaside escape while incorporating contemporary elegance. The design will focus on creating a light, airy atmosphere that reflects the tranquility of the coast, ideal for a family looking to unwind and enjoy their coastal home. The palette will include soft blues, sandy beiges, and crisp whites, with natural textures and nautical accents adding to the relaxed yet refined feel.

Conceptualization and Planning

Design Process

The initial phase involves developing a comprehensive design concept that aligns with the client's vision of a coastal retreat. This includes creating mood boards, selecting a cohesive color palette, and identifying key design elements such as textures and finishes. Detailed planning ensures that every aspect of the design complements the overall theme, from the choice of materials to the layout of each space.

Implementation and Styling

Design Process

Following the conceptual phase, the implementation stage focuses on executing the design plan. This includes sourcing and installing furniture, fixtures, and decor that align with the coastal theme. Attention is given to achieving a seamless integration of living and kitchen areas, as well as crafting serene and stylish bedroom and bathroom spaces. Final styling touches, such as art pieces and decorative accents, are added to enhance the cohesive look and feel of the home.

Client Feedback

Their attention to detail and understanding of our needs were exceptional. We couldn't be more thrilled with the outcome!

Elegant Urban Loft

Client

Emma Johnson

Service

Full Interior Design

Date

July 2002

The Idea

Re-imagining the Future of Space

The concept for the Elegant Urban Loft revolves around blending modern urban aesthetics with cozy, welcoming touches. The goal is to create a space that feels both sophisticated and comfortable, making it an ideal living environment for a young professional in the city. The design will incorporate clean lines, neutral colors, and strategic use of textures to add depth and interest.

— We started with

Kitchen Design

The kitchen will be designed to be both functional and stylish. It will include custom cabinetry in a matte finish, a marble countertop, and high-end stainless steel appliances. An island with bar stools will provide additional seating and workspace. Open shelving will be used to display dishware and decorative items, adding a personal touch. The color scheme will be consistent with the living area, using neutral colors to maintain a cohesive look.

— The Fundamentals

Bedroom Design

The bedroom will be a sanctuary of relaxation. A platform bed with a tufted headboard will be the focal point, accompanied by nightstands with sleek, modern lamps. Soft, luxurious bedding in neutral tones will ensure comfort. A large wardrobe with mirrored doors will provide ample storage space while making the room feel larger. A reading nook with a comfortable chair and a small bookshelf will offer a quiet retreat.

— Product Design

Bathroom Design

The bathroom will combine functionality with elegance. Key features will include a walk-in shower with a glass enclosure, a floating vanity with ample storage, and a large mirror with integrated lighting. The use of natural stone tiles will add a touch of luxury. The color palette will remain neutral, with warm lighting to create a spa-like atmosphere.
