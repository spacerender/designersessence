Art Direction - Design

Elegant Urban Loft

Client

Emma Johnson

Service

Full Interior Design

Date

July 2002

The Idea

Re-imagining the Future of Space

The concept for the Elegant Urban Loft revolves around blending modern urban aesthetics with cozy, welcoming touches. The goal is to create a space that feels both sophisticated and comfortable, making it an ideal living environment for a young professional in the city. The design will incorporate clean lines, neutral colors, and strategic use of textures to add depth and interest.

— We started with

Kitchen Design

The kitchen will be designed to be both functional and stylish. It will include custom cabinetry in a matte finish, a marble countertop, and high-end stainless steel appliances. An island with bar stools will provide additional seating and workspace. Open shelving will be used to display dishware and decorative items, adding a personal touch. The color scheme will be consistent with the living area, using neutral colors to maintain a cohesive look.

— The Fundamentals

Bedroom Design

The bedroom will be a sanctuary of relaxation. A platform bed with a tufted headboard will be the focal point, accompanied by nightstands with sleek, modern lamps. Soft, luxurious bedding in neutral tones will ensure comfort. A large wardrobe with mirrored doors will provide ample storage space while making the room feel larger. A reading nook with a comfortable chair and a small bookshelf will offer a quiet retreat.

— Product Design

Bathroom Design

The bathroom will combine functionality with elegance. Key features will include a walk-in shower with a glass enclosure, a floating vanity with ample storage, and a large mirror with integrated lighting. The use of natural stone tiles will add a touch of luxury. The color palette will remain neutral, with warm lighting to create a spa-like atmosphere.
